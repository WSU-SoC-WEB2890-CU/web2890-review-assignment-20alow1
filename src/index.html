faq

Frequently asked questions; get knowledge before committing

I charge $50 per hour that I spend working on your website. This includes any additional time to take photos or design graphics for your website. So the total cost of your website will vary depending on how complex of a project it is.

The time it takes to finish your website will depend on how complex it is. If it's only one page, it may take 1-2 weeks. If it's a multiple-page website, it may take 1-4 months. It's best to contact me several months before you want your website launched so I have time to finish it, as I may have multiple projects I'm working on. However, if you need something faster, we may be able to work something out at a higher hourly cost.

Before I start coding your website, I will design a mockup of what it will look like, and I can walk through it with you. If there's anything you don't like at that point, you should let me know and I will adjust it to your liking. The more you communicate with me early on, the more problems we can avoid later (and less you have to pay). I will revise it as much as needed to get the website looking and functioning the way you want it to. If you still decide you don't like it and don't want to continue with me, I'll still charge you for the hours I spent working on it.

I will write some basic text for you, like links and headers, but the majority of the text will need to come from you, if possible. I work with many customers who are specialized in areas I am not, so if I tried writing the text, it would take more time, money, and I'd be contacting you very often to check what I wrote. If you don't feel you are capable of writing the text, we can talk about hiring a separate copywriter to write the content for you.
